## Workflows
* [Workflow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scientific_workflow_system) is a series of computational or data manipulation steps.
* "Workflows are becoming the paradigm of choice for HPC applications."

### Como reproduzir workflows?
* [Mini-Apps](https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.18073): proxies that faithfully replicate the core computational and data-flow patterns of full workflows. [Case study](https://arxiv.org/html/2506.11100v1).
* [Nexflow](https://www.nextflow.io/).
